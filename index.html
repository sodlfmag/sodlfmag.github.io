Mateus Asato

Mateus Asato is a Brazilian guitar player who has been considered to the media one of the "most influential guitar players" of this modern era.

His name is included on

Guitar World Magazine’s list of
the TOP 10 Guitarists of the Decade (2020),
#3 TOP Guitarist Right Now (via Total Guitar 2020),

and his latest appearance was on Guitar World Magazine in 2022, being on number #14 of the list of ‘Hottest Guitar Players In The World’.
